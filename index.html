About Logix Space

Who We Are

LOGIX SPACE TECHNOLOGIES Pvt Ltd (LST) is a dynamic technology firm providing innovative solutions to help clients thrive in competitive environments.

Founded in 2014 by young entrepreneurs from University of Kerala and incubated at Startup Village Ernakulam, we've grown into a full-service technology provider with specialized expertise in AI solutions.

We don't just build technology - we create intelligent systems that solve real business challenges.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional value through innovative technology solutions powered by AI research.

Our Vision

To be a leader in AI-driven digital transformation for businesses worldwide.

Our Values

Innovation, Excellence, Customer Focus, and Continuous Learning.
